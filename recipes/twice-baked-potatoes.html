Twice-Baked Potatoes

Cheesy and savory twice-baked potatoes.

Like most of you, I'm sure, I've always had a soft spot for comfort food, and these twice-baked potatoes definitely deserve that title. Buttery mashed potatoes, cheese, sour cream, BACON. All in one compact dish? What more could you ask for!? Fun fact: the first time I made these I accidentally added double the amount of cheese and sour cream, since I was halving the recipe. I was so mad at myself at first, but my wife encouraging me, saying she was sure they'd still be delicious. Spoiler alert; they were. And now I always make them with extra cheese and sour cream, because let's be honest here, more the merrier in that department.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 8 green onions, sliced, divided

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 350ยบ F
  2. Bake potatoes in the preheated oven until tender, about 1 hour, depending on the size of your potatoes. Set potatoes aside until cool enough to handle.
  3. Meanwhile, place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
  4. Slice potatoes in half lengthwise and scoop the flesh into a large bowl; save the skins
  5. Add sour cream, milk, butter, salt, pepper, 1/2 cup cheese, and 1/2 of the green onions to the potato; mix with a hand mixer until well blended and creamy.
  6. Spoon the mixture into the potato skins; top each with remaining cheese, green onions, and bacon.
  7. Return potatoes to the preheated oven and continue baking until the cheese is melted, about 15 minutes. Serve hot!
