Chicken Pot Pie

Delicious chicken pot pie with a buttery, flakey crust

Growing up I loved those frozen chicken pot pies you'd get at the store, but I knew they were super unhealthy and loaded with saturated and trans fats. As an adult I wanted to try and recreated a chicken pot pie just as delicious as I remembered the frozen versions being, and boy did I find just the recipe! This chicken pot pie is thick, creamy, full of flavor, and the crust is SO flakey and buttery.

Ingredients

  • 1 Homemade Pie Crust, split in 2
  • 4 cups of uncooked chicken, shredded (Tip: Use a whole rotisserie chicken!)
  • 6 tbsp of salted butter
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 medium carrots, thinly sliced
  • 8oz white or crimini mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup of all purpose flour
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt, plus kosher salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper, plus more to garnish
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup of parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten, for egg wash

Steps

  1. In a dutch oven or large sauce pan, melt 6 tbsp of butter. Add diced onions and carrots and saute for 8 minutes over medium high heat until soft.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and minced garlic. Saute another 5 minutes until mushrooms are softened.
  3. Add 1/3 cup flour and stir constantly for 2 minutes until the flour particles are completely mixed in. Add chicken stock, and 1/2 cup heavy cream, then bring to a simmer and cook 1 minute or until mixture has thickened. Add 2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
  4. Add shredded, cooked chicken, frozen peas, and 1/4 cup chopped parsley. Stir to combine then remove from heat and cool slightly while you roll out the pie dough. Roll 1 chilled pie dough disk into a 12" diameter circle. Carefully transfer it into a deep 9" pie dish. Spoon the pie filling into the dish.
  5. Roll the second disk of pie dough into a 10" diameter circle and place over the pie filling. Fold the excess dough behind the bottom crust then crimp the pie dough together to seal. Use a sharp paring knife to cut 5 small slits in the top to allow steam to escape. Brush the top of the crust with the beaten egg and sprinkle lightly with coarse salt and pepper.
  6. Bake at 425ยบ for 30-35 minutes, or until top crust is golden brown. If edges are browning too fast, cover with a pie shield or make a shield by cutting strips of foil and wrapping them around the edges of the pie. Once out of the oven, rest for 15 minutes to cool slightly before slicing.
