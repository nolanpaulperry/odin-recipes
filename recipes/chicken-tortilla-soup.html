Chicken Tortilla Soup

Spicy, flavorful chicken tortilla soup - perfect for a chilly fall evening!

I'm sure everybody says this about their recipes but this is by far the best chicken tortilla soup recipe I've ever made. There was a restaurant I used to go to with my family that had the best chicken tortilla soup. They unfortunately closed back in 2020. Thank you covid... But this recipe is JUST like the soup they would make! I'm so glad I'm able to make it at home, and I want to share the love with you all!

Ingredients

  • 32 oz Chicken Stock (1 whole box)
  • 1 can of diced green chilis
  • 1 can of fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of frozen corn
  • 1-2lbs of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp of Homemade Taco Seasoning
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Shredded jack cheese

Steps

  1. Place the chicken stock, diced green chilis, diced tomatoes, frozen corn, and chicken thighs in a slow cooker
  2. Add the homemade taco seasoning and mix it in well
  3. Set the slow cooker on low for 8-10 hours
  4. After 8-10 hours shred the chicken with two forks, serve up and add cheese and cilantro to taste
